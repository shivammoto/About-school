Support for transient families

Moving to a new country can be challenging but don’t fret! We provide support to help you settle in and form a social circle and also help prepare for transition in case you are moving out.

Director Vanita Uppal OBE Appointed as FOBISIA Chair

In addition to running TBS with outstanding leadership for nearly a decade, our Director, Vanita Uppal OBE, is now also the Chair of FOBISIA. This prestigious appointment reflects her exceptional dedication to fostering excellence in education.

Samaara Bhardwaj wins at the Outstanding Cambridge Learner Awards

Samaara Bhardwaj, Year 12, was awarded Top in India for Cambridge IGCSE Literature in English at the Outstanding Cambridge Learner Awards for her exceptional performance. She gained the highest mark in India.

TBS partners with UCL to launch Summer School

TBS has partnered with University College London (UCL) to launch their inaugural UCL India Summer School. The programme will take place at TBS in June 2024 and will give students a taste of what it’s like to study at a world-leading UK university. The Summer School will be open to pre-university students across India.

TBS amongst Top 3 Private Schools in India

TBS has been named amongst the Top 3 Private Schools in India in the Global Schools Index. This also puts us amongst the Best 125 Private Schools internationally.

Udit Singhal, class of 2020, makes it to Forbes 30 under 30

Udit Singhal became the youngest member of the Forbes India 30 under 30 list in 2023 for pioneering an innovative and environmentally friendly technology that converts used glass bottles into sand through his solution called Glass2Sand.

TBS Hosts its First-Ever CareerX Takeover

TBS organised CareerX Takeover for Years 7 and 8 students, where they extended their learning beyond the classroom. Over two days, 30 speakers from a diverse range of fields such as sports, art, business, design, theatre, IT, culinary arts, and journalism addressed the students.

TBS Awarded Diamond Safety Shield Certificate

TBS was awarded the Diamond Safety Shield Certificate—the highest category in the programme—by Momentum, for maintaining impeccable safety standards and providing a secure environment to both students and staff.

Adya Uppal Awarded the Kurt Hahn Prize

Adya Uppal, Class of 2024, was awarded the coveted Kurt Hahn Prize by Round Square for her exceptional work in raising awareness on cancer. Adya was the first student from TBS to receive the award.
