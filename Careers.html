Please note that The British School does not charge any fee at any stage of the recruitment process (application, interview, meeting, process, training or any other fees). Please do not reply to any such mail or solicitation by phone. Kindly inform us at recruiter@british-school.org in case any such mail or phone call is received by you.

Job Disclaimer and Notification

It has come to our notice that certain unscrupulous individuals/placement agencies, falsely claiming to be associated with The British School New Delhi, may approach job aspirants and entice them with misleading employment offers for a fee. These fraudulent individuals/agencies may demand money by way of fees, charges, travel expense, training expenses, etc. from aspiring candidates for securing employment with us and may even use our trademarks.

We wish to notify that we do not demand or charge any fee/charges from aspiring candidates for considering their candidature or at any time during the recruitment process, including for training purposes, nor have we authorised any individual, agency or firm recruiting candidates for us to do so. Any person seeking employment with The British School New Delhi needs to be cautious and not trust any agency or individual, claiming to be our representative, and charging fees for providing/securing employment with us.

We wish to advise everyone seeking employment with The British School New Delhi to kindly note the following:

  1. Do not respond to any unsolicited job offers from individuals/ agencies claiming to be our employees or authorised agents who are demanding a fee/charge for job application. Please note that any communication demanding money that appears to originate from The British School New Delhi is false.
  2. Do not make any payment to any individual/ agency for securing employment with us. We do not charge anything from the candidate seeking employment with us nor do we authorise anyone to do so on our behalf.
  3. Do not disclose any personal and confidential information without proper verification and checking the authenticity of the communication. We never ask for bank account or other financial information at any stage of the recruitment process. In case of the slightest suspicion about the source and credentials of any individual/ agency claiming to be our representative, you are requested to immediately report the same to our office at recruiter@british-school.org.
  4. Beware of fake email ids or communication issued on fake letter heads.
